PROMETHEUS
EPISODE VI
Prometheus is the annual Technical Fest of IECSE. This is the sixth time the fest will be organized by the club. The technical extravaganza will begin on the 24th of March and will continue till the 26th. We have planned eight events for Prometheus Episode VI which includes both technical and non technical competitions. In the previous years we have seen participation in mass numbers in all events and we expect an even greater turn up this time.

It's 'Reimagining Reality' this time. So as we say,
Let the games begin!
EVENTS
HawkEye
HawkEye is an online scavenging hunt where you match wits against hundreds of participants solving cryptic puzzles and collecting small but vital clues to advance through the levels. HawkEye
Online Coding
Enjoyed participating in competitions on Hackerrank and CodeChef? Immerse yourself in mind-boggling coding questions on an online judge for 3 days straight.
Artem
Love designing and drawing? Test your graphic design skills in this 2-round event.
Bamboozled
MIT's first ever game show! Again. This time, bigger, better, crazier. Come join the madness and be part of an event you'll never forget.
Man vs Machine
Machines have overpowered humans and men must use their coding skills to break away from the shackles of the machines and save mankind.
Final Frontier
An event where competitive coding meets space exploration. Explore different civilizations and planets and conquer them using your coding skills.
Paper Presentation
The paper presentation is an opportunity to every participant to showcase an idea that can change it's current perception. There is a chance for every idea to be showcased and presented to the world.
Enigma
First ever platform independent Dev event. Think you can build a functional application? Come, prove your skills.
